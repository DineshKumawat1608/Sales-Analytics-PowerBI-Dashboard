# 📊 Sales Analytics Power BI Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze **sales performance, customer behavior, and product performance** through KPI-driven visualizations and dynamic filtering.

The solution combines executive-level business metrics with customer and product analytics, enabling decision-makers to monitor sales trends, identify high-value customers, evaluate product performance, and explore detailed business insights from a centralized reporting interface.

---

# 📌 Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Customer Dashboard

Provides customer-focused analytics to understand purchasing behavior and customer value.

### Highlights

- Total Customers
- Total Sales
- Average Sales per Customer
- Average Orders per Customer
- At-Risk Customers
- Revenue by Customer Segment
- Revenue by Country
- Revenue by Age Group
- Orders vs Sales by Customer Segment
- Customer Information Table

---

## 2️⃣ Product Dashboard

Provides product-level insights to evaluate business performance.

### Highlights

- Total Revenue
- Total Products
- Quantity Sold
- Average Selling Price
- Gross Margin %
- Revenue by Category
- Quantity Sold by Category
- Top 15 Products by Revenue
- Revenue vs Quantity Analysis
- Cost vs Selling Price Analysis
- Product Information Table

---

# ✨ Interactive Features

### Dashboard Navigation

- Switch seamlessly between Customer and Product dashboards
- Consistent navigation experience

### Dynamic Filtering

Filter reports using:

- Country
- Gender
- Customer Segment
- Age Group
- Product Category
- Product Subcategory
- Product Name
- Product Segment
- Recency
- Lifespan

### Interactive Analysis

- Cross-filtering across visuals
- Dynamic KPI updates
- Drill-down analysis
- Searchable detail tables
- Responsive report interactions

# 📊 Business Insights

The dashboard helps answer questions such as:

### Customer Analytics

- Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
- Which countries contribute the most sales?
- Which age groups generate the highest revenue?
- How many customers are considered at risk?
- How do customer segments compare in sales and order frequency?

### Product Analytics

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which categories contribute most to sales?
- How does quantity sold relate to revenue?
- Which products have the highest selling price?
- What is the overall gross margin?
